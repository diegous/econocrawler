Such proposals have ultimately favoured large-vertically integrated farmers and multinational corporations. An alternative policy proposal is presented, which suggests changes in the land, tenure system, the production structure of the small farm areas in Misiones, and the yerba mate marketing system. These changes are articulated in such a way enhances the economic and political feasibility of the policy proposal.</p>[/vc_column_text][vc_empty_space height="10px"][/vc_column][/vc_row]
