In the second one, the superb effort that Economic Theory has devoted to equilibrium analysis and the little attention that analysis of the behaviour of far from equilibrium systems has deserved are remarked.In this respect, it is argued that non-equilibrium Economic Theory would precisely allow to analyze the processes of change, viewing instability as a source of modification in the behaviour of systems, just as it happened in non equilibrium Thermodynamics.</p>[/vc_column_text]

Those banks who participate in none of these debt reduction alternatives, generally must commit to provide new money to the creditor country. The aim of this paper is to present a model of valuation of menu items for the Brady proposal that includes debt reducing and new money alternatives. This paper analyses a class of liquidity-in-advance model economies to show that there are several features, especially the technology available to generate liquidity (which is a key element when modeling highly inflationary economies) that can make these two variables misleading indicators of the transitory or permanent nature of a stabilization program.</p>[/vc_column_text]
