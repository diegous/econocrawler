They group themselves around two centers who give birth to two economic schools: to the North, Mexico and to the South, Chuquisaca. Their study covers a period of almost three hundred years. From their works we infer that the study of scholastic Economics has been very intensive in the "New World". The development of the subjective theory of value and the theory of the formation and variation of prices has been as strong as in Spain.
